# Sites & Funnels Guide

Welcome to WebWaka Sites & Funnels! This powerful tool lets you build professional websites and conversion funnels for your clients—no coding required.

## What is Sites & Funnels?

Sites & Funnels is a website builder designed for Partners like you. It allows you to:

- **Create Websites**: Build beautiful, responsive websites for your clients
- **Build Funnels**: Create multi-step conversion funnels to capture leads
- **Use Templates**: Start with industry-specific templates and customize them
- **Track Results**: Monitor page views, conversions, and visitor behavior
- **Custom Domains**: Connect client domains for white-label delivery

## Getting Started

### Creating Your First Site

1. Navigate to **Partner Portal → Sites**
2. Click **Create Site**
3. Enter a name and URL slug
4. Choose a template (optional) or start from scratch
5. Use the block-based editor to build pages
6. Preview on desktop and mobile
7. Publish when ready

### Creating a Funnel

1. Go to **Partner Portal → Funnels**
2. Click **Create Funnel**
3. Select your conversion goal (Lead Capture, Booking, etc.)
4. Add funnel steps (landing page, form, thank you)
5. Customize each step's content
6. Activate the funnel

## How AI Helps (and Its Limits)

### What AI Can Do

- **Generate Headlines**: Create compelling headlines based on your industry
- **Write Body Copy**: Draft professional content for sections
- **Suggest CTAs**: Recommend effective call-to-action text
- **SEO Optimization**: Generate meta titles and descriptions
- **Localization**: Adapt content for different markets

### What AI Cannot Do

- **Auto-publish**: All AI content requires your approval
- **Make Legal Claims**: AI won't generate legal or medical advice
- **Promise Results**: No "guaranteed" or misleading claims
- **Replace You**: AI assists, but you make final decisions

### Using AI Effectively

1. Provide context (industry, audience, tone)
2. Review and edit AI suggestions
3. Always approve before using
4. Track AI usage in your quota

## FAQ

**Q: How many sites can I create?**
A: Your plan determines your site limit. Check your entitlements in Partner Settings.

**Q: Can my clients edit their sites?**
A: Yes, if you grant them access. You control permission levels.

**Q: How do custom domains work?**
A: Add a domain in Site Settings, then point DNS to our servers. SSL is automatic.

**Q: Is there a page limit per site?**
A: Yes, based on your plan. Typically 10-20 pages per site.

**Q: Can I export my sites?**
A: Analytics can be exported. Full site export is on our roadmap.
